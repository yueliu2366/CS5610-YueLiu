Experiment Description:

This is my first try out of nodejs. It requires express module. I created a folder called static at the root folder and built two static html files under the folder. By definding app.use(express.static(__dirname + '/static')),we can hit the two websites under static folder by url: localhost:3000/index.html and url: localhost:3000/hello.html

Here is the sinppet of JS file:


var express = require('express')
var app = express()
app.use(express.static(__dirname + '/static'));
